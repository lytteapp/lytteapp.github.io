Listen & Learn

Lytte means to listen, and that's the idea behind this app: Listen to new words, phrases, and sentences regularly till those are part of your memory.

Configure & Customize

In addition to the 22000+ seeded words and sentences in 31 different languages, the app allows you to add new words/phrases/audios/images and modify the existing data.

Test & Analyze

Validate your progress using various tests and quizzes. The app automagically modifies your learning and listening plans depending on your results to improve your performance.
