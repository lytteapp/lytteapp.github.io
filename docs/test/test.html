Test

You can navigate to the test screen either from your Dashboard or lists screen. If you have selected the options to download all assets at the language selection screen, then the app will take you directly to the testing screen. Otherwise, the app will verify if all the assets are available or not for the selected list.

If the assets (Images, Audios) are not locally available, the app will show a confirmation popup message: asking you to download all the required assets. Asset download is a one-time process.

The test screen is divide into two tabs :

Reading

In the reading tab, the app shows the word/sentence with four choices.

Click on any choice to select it. If you click on the right choice, the background color of the option will turn green, and the app will take you to the next screen. If you click on any wrong option, then the background color will turn red.

You can navigate to the next/previous question by clicking on the next/previous buttons.

Result

On the last question, the app will show you a result button. You can view the details of the test on the result screen. You can also navigate to the test results from the dashboard and list screen.

Listening

The listening screenplays audio and does not show the word. When you navigate to a question, the app plays the word only once. If you want to listen to the word again, then click on the play button.

Sentences

The test screens for sentences look and behave as word screens. The only difference is that the default delay time between sentences is higher than words.
